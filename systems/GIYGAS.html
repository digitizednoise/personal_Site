GIYGAS
Audio / Visual System
⚠ HIGH INTENSITY FLASHING WARNING ⚠
System displays an intense red storm of visuals, reacting to audio in real-time within Touchdesigner. GIYGAS will react in a variety of different ways; cycling through the different visuals and reacting accordingly to music.
Incoming audio is split up into lows, mids, and highs; with system listening and reacting to individual parts and sending CHOP values to GIYGAS.
Technologies: TouchDesigner | Python | Logic Pro X
